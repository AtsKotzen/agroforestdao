Stigmergy

Stigmergy is an indirect coordination mechanism, through the environment, between agents or actions. The principle is that the trace left in the environment by an individual action stimulates the performance of a successive action by the same or another agent.

How to participate

Mentor

If you already dedicate your life to managing agroforestry systems, teaching courses and hosting volunteers, you can join our Mentor channel where you will get to know web3 and our community. Turn your home into a Sintrópico Laboratory, create a profile and share a schedule of regular meetings and educational events. Learn to create a profile and mint NFTs with monthly content about your systems and value your knowledge and dedication.
Open guest list for Mentor Passports.

Apprentice

Join the Agroforest + Web3 learning journey and learn how to fund your studies by producing monthly NFT content and building your reputation. Volunteers and Nomads are welcome! After the learning journey, you can earn a Mentor Badge and Rooted Club Passport.

Collaborator

If you own land, you can offer a plot of your land to serve as a learning ground for mentors and mentees. Future partnerships can be created with the support of the Rooted Community.

Supporter

Purchase the Rooted Club passport that gives you access to our members-only channel, where you can participate in governance, book stays, experiences, courses, workshops, browse the profiles of other members, find experts, interns and future partners for joint agroforestry ventures . If you're a traveler who wants to follow a global seasonal calendar while being welcomed by other deep-rooted communities, this channel is for you.
Participate by purchasing one of our NFT's to access the benefits.

Teacher

If you are recognized for having dedicated many decades of your life to the establishment of agroforestry systems, you will be recognized as a Master and will have an advisory role to play in the community, in addition to being invited to regional events. We want to allow you to earn carbon credits for the forests you manage.


Companies

If you are a food supply chain professional and need partnerships to scale your work, then this role is for you. You can access channels discussing partnership agreements with other network members and get support to improve your food processing infrastructure and get paid for your regenerative service, while partners share royalties from product sales and ecosystem credits on the long term.
